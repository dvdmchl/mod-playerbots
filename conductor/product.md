# Product Guide: mod-playerbots

## Initial Concept
`mod-playerbots` is an [AzerothCore](https://www.azerothcore.org/) module that adds player-like bots to a server, providing a realistic MMO experience by allowing bots to level up, form parties, and participate in raids and battlegrounds.

## Product Vision
To create the most realistic and performant player-bot experience for AzerothCore, allowing server administrators to populate their worlds with intelligent, self-sufficient characters that behave like real players.

## Target Audience
- **Server Administrators:** Who want to populate low-population servers or provide a solo-friendly experience.
- **Developers:** Who want to extend bot behaviors and integrate new AI strategies.
- **Players:** Who enjoy interacting with bots, forming parties, and completing content that would otherwise require multiple human players.

## Core Goals
- **Realism:** Bots should mimic player behavior, including movement, combat, questing, and social interaction.
- **Performance:** Support thousands of concurrent bots with minimal impact on server resources.
- **Configurability:** Provide extensive settings to tune bot behavior and server population.
- **Stability:** Maintain a crash-free experience on all supported platforms (Ubuntu, Windows, macOS).

## Key Features
- **Random Bots:** Bots that wander the world, complete quests, and behave like players.
- **Alt Bots:** The ability for players to log in their own alt characters as bots.
- **Party & Raid Support:** Bots capable of running complex dungeon and raid content with tailored strategies.
- **Battleground & PvP:** Bots participating in world PvP and structured battlegrounds.
- **Chat Command System:** An intuitive command-based interface for controlling bots.
- **Addon Integration:** Support for in-game UIs to manage bot groups.
